Safety & Security: More Than Tint

Help hold glass together during break‑ins, impacts, or storms with safety film.

How security film works

Security film bonds to glass, increasing its resistance to shattering. If glass breaks, the film helps retain fragments, slowing forced entry and reducing injury risk.

Use cases

  • Storefronts and display windows
  • Schools and common areas
  • Homes with large patio doors and sidelights

Installation & anchors

For the best performance, films can be paired with attachment systems that secure the film to window frames.

FAQ

Is it the same as bulletproof glass? No. Security film strengthens existing glass but does not make it bulletproof.
