Privacy Films for Homes & Offices

Keep daylight, block views, and upgrade style with frosted and decorative films.

Where privacy film works best

  • Street-facing living rooms and entry sidelights
  • Bathrooms and interior glass doors
  • Offices and conference rooms
  • Storefronts needing branding + privacy

Popular styles

  • Frosted: Clean, modern look with diffused light.
  • Matte/Whiteout: Maximum privacy; blocks views entirely.
  • Patterns & stripes: Adds design while controlling sight lines.
  • Custom graphics: Logos and wayfinding for businesses.

Care & durability

Quality films resist peeling and discoloration. Clean with non‑abrasive products and soft cloths to keep them looking new.

FAQ

Can I still see out? Frosted and patterned films blur details but keep light. One‑way privacy requires specific lighting conditions and is not guaranteed at night.
